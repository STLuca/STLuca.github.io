MunchBunch

An app for restaurant owners and restaurant customers. Restaurant owners have the ability to add data about their restaurant such as name, cuisine and location along with creating the restaurant menu. Users can find local restaurants and use the restaurant menu to make their order. Restaurant owners can see these orders and update them for the user in realtime e.g. from pending -> cooking -> cooked.
